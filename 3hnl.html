Nestled in the heart of Calamba City, Laguna, Pepper Food House is conveniently situated near the Calamba City Plaza and the historic Bahay ni Rizal, honoring our national hero. This charming eatery, owned by a delightful couple, is tucked away on San Juan Road, Brgy. San Juan.
Since 2015, Pepper Food House has been a beacon of delectable comfort food, earning its reputation as a local favorite. Our mission is to bring you affordable yet utterly satisfying dishes that will leave your taste buds yearning for more.
